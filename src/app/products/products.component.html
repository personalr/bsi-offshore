PRODUCTS

We deliver a range of products to the rig industry, i.e.:

Wooden furniture

Wooden furniture from our own partly owned company HBH Innredning. These are typical furniture for cabins, public crew area, mess rooms, offices and so on. Products like beds, cabinets, tables and special designed furniture.

Steel furniture

Typical steel furniture like bunk beds for cabins, turnable beds or pullman beds.

Galley equipment

Galley equipment from all the leading producers of ship & offshore quality equipment, like Metos, Miele, Electrolux, Beha-Hedo and several more. Products like stainless steel benches, galley ranges and convection ovens.

Mess room equipment

We produce our own custom made mess room equipment either in wood or solid surface like Corian. We deliver refrigerated or heated benches from leading producers, as well as all equipment like drinking dispensers and coffee machines.

Laundry Machinery

Laundry machinery from all the leading producers of ship & offshore quality equipment, like Metos, Miele and Electrolux.

Insulation

Insulation products like Sea Rox (Rockwool) and Glava Marine & Offshore.

Fire rated doors

A range of doors from internal doors to exteral A-class and weather tight doors, as well as heavy duty offshore doors.

Wall and ceiling panel system

A wide range of wall and ceiling systems, for cabins, corridors, wheel-house, lounges etc.

Floor

Quality Maine & Offshore products, like Weber concrete and Sikafloor, epoxy, tiles, shipfloor and vinyl.
