HSE

We have established and documented a formal system for managing health, safety and environment (HSE). Our HSE-system complies with the applicable Norwegian laws and regulations. The system reflects a clear expression of commitment from the management of the company.

Our overall goals concerning HSE are:

  • We shall not have any damages or accidents to people
  • Our sickness leave shall be substantial lower than the national Norwegian average
  • We shall not have damages to goods and materials
  • We shall have a good working environment
